How It Works

A transparent look at the technology powering TruthLens.

01
📝

Text Preprocessing

Your input is cleaned—lowercased, stripped of HTML, and normalized—to prepare it for machine analysis.

02
🔢

TF-IDF Vectorization

Words are converted into numerical vectors using Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency, giving more weight to meaningful, unique terms.

03
🤖

Logistic Regression

A trained classifier evaluates the vectorized text and outputs a probability for each class: Real or Fake.

04
📊

Confidence & Explanation

The top TF-IDF weighted words from your text are surfaced as the explanation, giving you a transparent view of the decision.

About TruthLens

TruthLens is an open-source fake news detection tool built as a demonstration of how classical NLP and machine learning can be applied to real-world information verification.

The model is trained on the ISOT Fake News Dataset — a curated collection of thousands of real and fake news articles, covering politics, world events, and more.
